Planning a trip to Maui? Check out Our Top 7 Things to Do in Maui!

Home to tropical breezes, white-sand beaches, and touristy Lahaina town, Maui’s got it all. They say if you only have time to visit one island, Maui’s the one. This island combines Hawaii’s arresting natural beauty with the ease and comfort of the mainland, making it the best place to have a care-free vacation. Explore the island’s breathtaking landscape, from the Iao Valley to the Hana Highway and Haleakala, or sink your toes into the sand at Ka’anapali Beach. If you’re looking for things to do in Maui, look no further. Top 7 list

Top 7 things to do in Maui!

1. Snorkel the Molokini Crater

The Molokini Crater is considered one of the best places to go snorkeling in all of Hawaii. Located just off the coast of South Maui, this crescent-shaped island crater is breathtaking.

2. Visit the Beach(DUH!)

Maui boasts over 30 miles of beautiful beaches. Your bound to find many favorites, each beach has unique properties to make it stand out, Do you like white sand? Huge waves? or do you want your kiddos to splash around without losing grandma? theres a beach for everything and everyone.

3. Learn to Surf!

Learn to surf with a surf school in Kihei or Lahaina! they provide everything you need to surf in one easy lesson! Each lesson includes an approximate 10-15 minute land lesson on the basics of surfing, and ocean safety. The rest of the lesson is spent in the water with your instructor! Youll be a pro in no time!

4. Road to Hana

The road to Hana is one of those Must Do adventures! with the 600-plus turns on this 64-mile road, your bound to see Maui in all of its glory, with The unique small towns,hole in the wall beaches and the otherworldly vines growing all around the road.

5. Sunrise on Haleakala

The sunrise on Haleakala is definitely a Must See! from the highest point on Maui your staring over the clouds at a truly unforgettable sunrise!

6. FOOD!

The Food on Maui is one of the main Reasons vacationers become locals! Its that good! Do you like fish? well Maui's got you covered! Want a burger? how about the best burger youve ever had? Even the food Trucks are top notch, so much so that they cater weddings!

7. Move there!

  1. Temperatures are ideal. It is never too hot – over 100 degrees Fahrenheit, nor is it ever colder than starting your breath to fog. Almost constant “trade winds” that keep fresh air blowing in across the island to disperse whatever pollution might have come from vehicles or power plants.
  2. The Hawaiian Islands are surrounded by deep blue, powerful ocean. The color is unlike anything I’ve seen elsewhere. There is a real power to the ocean here – it’s awesome in it’s power, it’s constant movement. Awesome things to do. Shopping, walking, picnics, sightseeing, visiting attractions like the volcanoes and historical sights like Pearl Harbor.
  3. Surfing, bodyboarding, bodysurfing, swimming, sailing, kayaking, snorkeling, diving, hobiecatting, windsurfing, parasailing, parasurfing, and kiteboarding (on skateboards) are all fun activities available. If you get bored here, you’ve got a problem! Climbing / hiking. There are over twelve mountain ridge hikes on Oahu alone. Peaks over 4,000 feet high are available on Oahu. On Maui you can climb Haleakala volcano up over 10,000 feet if you were really inspired.
  4. Hawaii is so diverse. The people, the food, the things to do, the cultures, the way of life, the whole atmosphere is different from anything you’ve ever experienced.
